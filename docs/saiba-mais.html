Your Auto Liker is a free browser extension (at the moment only for Google Chrome) that allows users to define channels to the lists 'Always like' and 'Always dislike'.

This way once configurated which channels to react, it is just needed to watch their videos and let the extension automatically like or dislike it.

The extension was built to help users and content creators. It makes a genuine action automatic, that will only take place once a video is watched from specific channels.

This way, the main intention is to make user's life easier, automating real reactions, that sometimes are forgotten even being so important for the content creators.

As far as real users are responsible for selecting their favorite and unfavorite channels and watch their videos, the intentions are not to pretend any video reaction, however, make an intentional reaction automated.

We believe that the extension is legitimate as it follows as stated on Youtube Policies page, about Fake Engagement : " We consider engagement to be legitimate when a human user’s primary intent is to interact with content free of coercion or deception.”

This was my first real project using Javascript and also my first browser extension. I did my best to keep the code clean and organized even though the lack of experience that may have impacted the quality of the code.

Anyway, here is the source code on my GitHub . Please feel encouraged to take a look and send your contribution.
